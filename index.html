Welcome to the Bonjour/What's Up? Weather App!

I was inspired to create a weather app so that I can look up the weather of my favorite cities. I also wanted to showcase the beautiful work of Vahram Muraytan whose book 100 postcards comparing "Paris vs New York" is one of my favorites.

You can enter the name of a city, such as "Paris" or "New York" and you will retrieve the city's weather information including Temperature, Wind and an overall Description. Then persuse through the Muraytan's whimsical illustrations.

Lastly, don't forget to fill out the form so that we can create more exciting comparisons of your favorites cities. Here's to more travel in 2021 and beyond! Bon voyage and safe travels:)
